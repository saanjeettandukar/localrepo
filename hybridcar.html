Hybrid Car

A hybrid car combines an internal combustion engine with an electric motor to deliver better fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. It’s designed to switch between or combine power sources depending on driving conditions, offering both performance and eco-friendliness.

  • Body Type: Sedan
  • Mileage: 22 km/l (approx.)
  • Horsepower: 150 HP combined (engine + electric motor)
  • Environmental Impact: Produces fewer CO₂ emissions and consumes less fuel, helping reduce air pollution and dependence on fossil fuels.
